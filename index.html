About ParageBragdon

Our Mission

At ParageBragdon, we're passionate about providing the highest quality hoof care products for horses. Our formulations are developed by equine specialists with years of experience in veterinary care and farriery.

All our products are made with natural ingredients, carefully selected for their effectiveness in promoting hoof health, preventing infections, and supporting overall equine wellness.

We understand that your horse's health is paramount, which is why we've dedicated ourselves to creating solutions that truly work.
